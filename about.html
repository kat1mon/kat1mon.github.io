About Writer's Block

Writer's Block is a website looking to create a place for collaborative writers to flourish. Instead of attempting to bend a platform to the needs of the writer, Writer's Block provides a simple, intuitive interface that allows writers to focus on what they actually want to do: write. To make this experience as comfortable as possible to all of our writers, Writer's Block has a code of conduct that all users are required to follow.

Code of Conduct

  1. Be respectful to all users. Do not harass, intimidate, or target other users.
  2. Content on the site may not be appropriate for all audiences. To maintain the safety of all users, Writer's Block strictly requires all users to be at least 18 years of age. Do not attempt to create an account if you are under 18 years of age.
  3. As a site promoting creativity, do not attempt to "police" others regarding the topics they write about. While this site does not allow discrimination amongst users, please understand that these topics may be covered in creative works on the site.
  4. Respect the intellectual property of others. Do not reproduce or distribute copyrighted material without permission.
  5. Do not post content that is illegal, harmful, or violates the rights of others. Do not post the original content of others without permission.
  6. Do not use the site for commercial purposes.
  7. Report any violations of this code of conduct to the site administrators. Do not attempt to be a "vigilante".
  8. Understand that the code of conduct may evolve over time, and that continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the current version.
  9. Failure to follow this code of conduct may result in account termination.
